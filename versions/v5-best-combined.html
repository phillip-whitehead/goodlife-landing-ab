We believe in full transparency. Here are the answers our guests ask most.

💲 Are there any hidden booking fees?

No. What you see is what you pay. We charge no booking fees on top of the nightly rate. We'll give you a fully itemized quote before any commitment.

📅 What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation on most properties. Your quote will clearly show the cancellation terms for each specific property before you commit.

📞 What if something goes wrong during our stay?

You'll have a direct line to our local team 24/7. We're not a faceless platform — we're a local Bahamas team who can physically show up if needed.

All properties include concierge planning, airport transfer coordination, local recommendations, and personal support throughout your stay.

Tell us your dates, group size, and preferences. We send you a curated shortlist. You pick, we handle everything else. No pressure, no commitment to inquire.

Absolutely. The Bahamas is one of the most visited destinations in the Caribbean. Our team has 15+ years of local knowledge and will advise you on the best islands for your travel style.
