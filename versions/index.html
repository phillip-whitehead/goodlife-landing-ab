Arsh Sanwarwala / ThrillX — CRO Principles Applied

Each initial version tests exactly one hypothesis. The weekly evolution combines live data with these principles to iterate continuously.

Principle 1

Above the fold is everything

Headline + hero tests produce 50%+ conversion swings. Tested in v2.

Principle 2

Lower-commitment CTAs win

"Learn More" beat "Book A Call" and drove 385% more booked calls. Tested in v4.

Principle 3

Fewer CTAs = better

Cutting from two CTAs to one produces measurable lifts. Applied in v2 and v4.

Principle 4

Address visitor anxieties directly

Pricing transparency, cancellation policy, what's included. Tested in v3.

Principle 5

Review mining

Real guest language in copy. Applied in v3/v5: "like a cool cousin who knows everyone."

Principle 6

Shorter forms

Remove every non-essential field. Tested in v4: name + email only.

Principle 7

One hypothesis per version

Isolate the variable, measure the result, combine the winners.

Framework

"Beauty and Brains"

Professional visual design + data-driven optimisation. Both matter together.
