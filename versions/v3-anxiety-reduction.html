No. What you see is what you pay. We charge no booking fees on top of the nightly rate. We'll give you a fully itemized quote before any commitment.

We offer flexible cancellation on most properties. Your quote will clearly show the cancellation terms for each specific property before you commit.

You'll have a direct line to our local team 24/7. We're not a faceless platform — we're a local Bahamas team who can physically show up if needed.
