About Surf House

There's a moment — early morning, bare feet on the pool deck, Moriah Harbour stretching out in front of you in shades of turquoise that don't seem real — when you understand why people keep coming back to Exuma. Surf House is where that moment happens.

"The kind of place that makes you rethink your priorities."

This four-bedroom beachfront home sits in the heart of Hartswell, a quiet corner of Great Exuma that locals love and tourists rarely find. The pool looks directly onto the harbour. Every bedroom has ocean views. There's a full kitchen for lazy cook-at-home nights and an outdoor dining deck for the evenings when the sunset demands your full attention.

It sleeps eight comfortably — six beds, four baths — which makes Surf House ideal for a family reunion, a group of friends who've been talking about this trip for years, or couples who want the space to spread out. Pair it with The Good Life's personal concierge and your whole Exuma itinerary is handled: fishing charters, bonefishing guides, private chef evenings, boat rentals, whatever makes a trip extraordinary.

Exuma — Bahamas' Best-Kept Secret

Most visitors never make it past Nassau. The ones who find Exuma tend not to go anywhere else.

🌊

The Water

The Exuma Cays produce some of the most photographed water in the world. 50 shades of blue-green, all of them real.

🎣

World-Class Fishing

Moriah Harbour flats are legendary among bonefishers. Tarpon, barracuda, snapper — ask your concierge to arrange a guide.

⛵

Boat to the Cays

Stocking Island, Chat 'N' Chill, Thunderball Grotto — day trips that take 20 minutes and feel like a different world.

🌴

George Town

5 minutes from Surf House. Waterfront restaurants, local markets, the real Bahamas — not a resort version of it.
