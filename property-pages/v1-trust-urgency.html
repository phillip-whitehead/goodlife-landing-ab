About Surf House

Welcome to Surf House — a luxurious beachfront retreat in the heart of Hartswell, Moriah Harbour, Exuma. This stunning four-bedroom home is made for those seeking the ultimate Bahamian escape: turquoise water outside your door, a private pool for lazy afternoons, and the kind of tranquility you can only find on one of the world's most beautiful islands.

With space for 8 guests, six beds and four bathrooms, Surf House is ideal for families, friend groups, and couples celebrating something special. Paired with The Good Life's personal concierge service, every detail of your stay is taken care of — from local fishing trips to private chef bookings.
